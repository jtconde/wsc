PWR in the Online Classroom

A complete Pressurized Water Reactor simulation on the cloud. In an industry first, WSC has developed an online interactable simulation for students to operate, learn, and see a working nuclear power plant respond to their actions.

Intelligent Tutoring System (ITS) Integration

ITS automates many of the routine instructor functions performed in conjunction with simulators. Lessons run automatically on the simulator, record student actions and simulator response, and offer varying degrees of online help when needed.
